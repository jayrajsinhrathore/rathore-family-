History of Dhandhal Rathore

Dhandhal Rathore was a prominent figure in the royal lineage of Rajasthan. Known for his leadership, valor, and contribution to the region’s prosperity, Dhandhal played a key role in maintaining the traditions of the Rathore dynasty. His descendants have continued to influence the culture, politics, and economy of the area for centuries, passing down the legacy of their ancestors.
